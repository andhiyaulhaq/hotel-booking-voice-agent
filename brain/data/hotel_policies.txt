# The Grand AI Hotel - Policies & Knowledge Base

## Check-In and Check-Out
- Check-in time is 3:00 PM.
- Check-out time is 11:00 AM.
- Late check-out up to 2:00 PM is available for an additional fee of $50, subject to availability.
- Guests must provide a valid government-issued ID and a credit card for incidentals at check-in.

## Pet Policy
- We are a pet-friendly hotel!
- A maximum of two pets (dogs or cats only) are allowed per room.
- There is a non-refundable pet fee of $75 per stay.
- Pets must be kept on a leash in public areas and are not allowed in the pool or spa areas.

## Parking
- Valet parking is available for $45 per night.
- Self-parking is available in the adjacent garage for $25 per night.
- EV charging stations are complimentary for guests using valet parking.

## Amenities
- The fitness center is open 24/7 on the 3rd floor.
- The rooftop pool is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.
- Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and public areas.
- Breakfast is served from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM in the Grand Dining Room (not included in standard room rate unless specified).

## Local Recommendations (Concierge)
- **Seafood:** The Ocean Grille is a 5-minute walk from the hotel and is highly recommended.
- **Museums:** The Natural History Museum is located 2 miles away, accessible via the metro station across the street.
- **Coffee:** Bean & Leaf Cafe is right next door, opening at 5:30 AM.
